How it works

Reference period. 2 cl pure alcohol per this length, counted only within this many minutes after each logged drink (timestamps on the log; long gaps pause accrual until the next entry). Pick 30–120 min. Change anytime.

Drank / allowed. (1× 5% 40 cl + 2 cl / your chosen minutes after each drink; pauses after gaps.) Shown at the top as cl and approximate beers.

“Beers” line. Uses 40 cl · 5,5% as the reference serving for those equivalents.

Pause note. When allowed stops increasing because you’re past the reference period after your last drink, the app adds a short log line so you can see when that happened.
